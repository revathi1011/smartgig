About Us

Smartgig is a platform being built to bridge the gap between what is taught in academics to what the Industry is expecting. At the same time students in Rural areas doesn’t have a platform to understand what is that is needed to make themselves equipped to get a job.With ever changing technology landscape and evolving technologies it is imperative to have a platform which helps college students to know about what next and get hands on before they deep dive once onboarded in tech companies. Our mission is not just training students on IT but to spread training’s across other areas like Manufacturing, Administration, Disaster management etc.. Baby steps are being taken but we are sure we will create a platform with world class lab in Tier-2 cities across India
